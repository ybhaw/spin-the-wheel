Spin The Wheel

A free, customizable random picker

Features

  • Local Storage: Your wheels are saved automatically in your browser
  • Custom Images: Add background images via URL or file upload
  • Color Themes: Choose from 25+ beautiful color schemes
  • Adjustable Speed: Set spin duration from instant to 1 hour
  • Sound Effects: Immersive audio feedback (can be disabled)
  • Remove Winner: Optionally remove winners after each spin
  • Multiple Wheels: Create different wheels with unique titles
